$$\Lambda \cdot \ell_P^2 = \Omega_\Lambda^{-1} \cdot C(60/120) = 2\,\Omega_\Lambda^{-1}$$

$$\frac{\log\alpha}{\log(\Lambda \cdot \ell_P^2)} \approx \frac{-2.13}{-121.7} \approx \frac{1}{57}$$
